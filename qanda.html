Frequently Asked Questions

How does the service work?

We connect you with local waste collection services. Submit your request via our platform, and our team schedules your pickup.

What types of waste do you collect?

Our service covers dry waste, wet waste, and electronic waste for responsible disposal.

How can I track my pickup request?

Once submitted, you can track your request status through your personal dashboard.
